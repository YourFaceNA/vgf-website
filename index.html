What is Video Game Festival?

🕹️

Beat Games

Players complete ("beat") as many video games as possible across a 4-day marathon event. Every genre, every platform — anything goes!

💰

Raise Money

All proceeds go to children's charities. Stream your runs, get donations, and show the world that gaming makes a difference.

🤝

Community First

VGF is built on friendship, fair play, and fun. New players and veterans alike are welcome — come hang, game, and give back.
